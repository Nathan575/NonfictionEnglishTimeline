Donald Trump

"A tape would surface of Donald Trump in an unguarded moment, bragging to a TV host in 2005 about sexually assaulting women, using language so lewd and vulgar that it put media outlets in a quandary about how to quote it without violating the established standards of decency" (Obama 408).

  • 1946

    Birth (1946)

    He was born in Queens, New York, to a family that was gaining wealth through an economic boom after World War II. His family was Protestant, giving him a traditional Christian ideological basis.

  • 1964

    Graduated from New York Military Academy High School (1964)

    For high school, his parents sent him to a private boarding school that had a focus on developing character and leadership, giving him a strong, privileged education.

  • 1968

    Graduated from the University of Pennsylvania (1968)

    While in college, he was able to gain experience working at his father’s business. He graduated with a degree in economics. After doing so, he was able to immediately become employed in his father’s company.

  • 1980

    Grand Hyatt Hotel renovation complete (1980)

    Trump’s first large project involved a massive renovation project that first attracted public attention. To do so, he took a large tax abatement, but while he ascribes this to his own skill, it was actually largely orchestrated by his father.

  • 1987

    Art of the Deal Published (1987)

    This semi-biography (that wasn’t actually written by Trump) was a major starting point for Trump’s rise to popularity. In it, his life and accomplishments are described with flattery, cementing his reputation as charming and business-savvy and making him name-famous. However, a lot of the book is exaggerated or false, as he was not a very successful businessman.

  • 1988

    Donald J. Trump Foundation Established (1988)

    This organization was intended to serve to donate money to charitable causes such as health care. However, the foundation was eventually shut down after numerous problems such as misuse of funds and tax evasion were discovered.

  • 2016

    Donald Trump Elected President of the U.S. (2016)

    After a contentious election, Trump was elected president. This came as a surprise to many, as he had no former political experience, and was a poor businessman (though many people thought otherwise, for example because of Art of the Deal). He pushed right-wing politics, advocating relatively isolationist policies that restricted immigration and exited major international agreements like the Paris Agreement. He also undid Barack Obama’s “Obamacare” healthcare program. His slogan was to “make America great again”, suggesting that the country had fallen from former glory, as opposed to the Obamas' perspective that the country had to overcome ongoing issues rooted in the past.
